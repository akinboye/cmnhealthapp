## About Cure My Nation Initiative

Cure My Nation Initiative is more than an organization — it is a movement born out of compassion, urgency, and an unwavering commitment to human dignity.

We exist because too many Nigerians continue to suffer in silence — patients denied quality healthcare, families burdened by preventable illnesses, young people deprived of access to meaningful education, and communities trapped in cycles of poverty. These realities are not just statistics to us; they are stories of real people, real pain, and real lost opportunities.

At Cure My Nation Initiative, we believe that every Nigerian life matters, and that access to quality healthcare, education, and economic opportunity is not a privilege — but a fundamental human right.

Cure My Nation Initiative is a Non-Profit organization established specifically to advocate for quality education, good healthcare service delivery and poverty eradication in Nigeria and Africa at large.

We are a voluntary organization registered under the Corporate Affairs Commission Act 1990. Our organization focuses mainly on three (3) Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) namely: Poverty eradication, good healthcare service delivery, and quality education since 2017.

The role of Cure My Nation Initiative is to strengthen health and educational sectors to perceive their own problems/needs, and to solve them through collective action and participation.

Our team of professionals comprises of Nurses, Physicians, Psychologists, Lawyers, I.T experts, Statisticians, and specialists in Physical Health education.

## Our Projects

### 1. Health Without Borders
The focus areas of this initiative are:
- Sensitization: Rural dwellers are sensitized on activities that trigger health degradation. The sensitization is done by visiting schools and communities.
- Voice: Addresses the need to make rural health more visible to key stakeholders such as the government, the media, and the general public.
- Policy: Entails the rural-proofing of existing and new policies/guidelines and sensitizing people about their rights in the healthcare arena.
- Tests: Rural dwellers are given opportunity to partake in free blood sugar and blood pressure tests.
- Implementation: Focuses on how issues that cause poor health service delivery in rural areas can be tackled by the government. Cure My Nation will monitor and gather evidence on these issues and give them to the relevant stakeholders to act upon.

### 2. Youths Sustainable Skills and Empowerment Program
The project goal is to empower rural youths with vocational skills in Digital marketing, graphics design, Adire and Batik Making, Shoe making, Cutting and sewing, Computer Technology and Paint Making. It is purposed at poverty alleviation by addressing unemployment through imparting vocational skills so that youths become active players in income generating activities.

### 3. Teachers' Fruits
Teachers' Fruits is the first teacher-students reunion platform in Africa initiated under the umbrella of Cure My Nation Initiative. Its obligation is to track, locate and appreciate teachers for their commitment to the teaching profession based on requests from their students.

### 4. COVID-19 Fund Monitoring System
Cure My Nation Initiative channelled its resources to monitor government expenditures on COVID-19 and identify red flags or opportunities for corruption; and in cases where funds are already diverted or mismanaged, ensure that the looted funds are adequately recovered and perpetrators brought to book.

## CMN Health Rights Platform — Overview

Cure My Nation (CMN) Health Rights Platform is a digital platform built by the Cure My Nation NGO to protect and empower patients across Nigeria. It gives every Nigerian — regardless of education level, location, or income — a simple, safe way to know their health rights, report abuse or mistreatment by hospitals, and get real-time support. The platform is available as a web application.

Mission Statement: Cure My Nation is more than a reporting app — it is a civic tool for health justice. It turns an isolated, frustrated patient into an informed, empowered citizen who knows their rights, can document violations, and can demand accountability.

---

## Problem Statement

Healthcare violations in Nigeria are widespread and underreported. Patients are routinely denied treatment, overcharged, mistreated, or kept in the dark about their own medical decisions. The key reasons violations go unaddressed include:

- Patients do not know their rights — Most people are unaware that a legally recognised Patient Bill of Rights exists.
- No accessible reporting channel — Reporting a hospital traditionally requires navigating slow and intimidating government bureaucracies.
- Vulnerable groups are most affected — Rural communities, women, the elderly, and low-income patients suffer disproportionately.
- Data is not tracked — Without collected data, advocates and policymakers cannot pressure governments to act.

CMN fills this gap by putting a reporting tool, a rights guide, an AI assistant, and live human support all in one place — accessible from a phone.

---

## Platform Features

### Feature 1: Patient Bill of Rights
Publishes Nigeria's 11 core patient rights in plain language, available in 10 languages including Yoruba, Hausa, Igbo, Pidgin, French, Arabic, Swahili, Fulfulde, and Portuguese.

### Feature 2: Violation Reporting
Registered users can file structured violation reports in minutes — selecting violation type, hospital name, description, and priority level. All reports are stored securely and tracked. Every report is assigned a unique tracking ID.

Violation types include:
- Denial of Treatment
- Medical Malpractice
- Overcharging / Over Charging
- Patient Neglect / Negligence
- Privacy Violation
- Mistreatment / Mistreated
- Infection Control

### Feature 3: Case Tracking Dashboard
Users monitor report progress in real time: Reported → In Progress → Resolved or On Hold. Personal statistics show total cases filed, resolved, and resolution rate.

### Feature 4: AI-Powered Health Chatbot
A built-in AI chatbot (powered by OpenAI) provides round-the-clock answers on symptoms, medications, patient rights, and guidance on experiencing mistreatment.

### Feature 5: Live Admin Chat
Users chat directly with a Cure My Nation support agent. Admins receive real-time sound-alert notifications and can manage all active conversations in a single panel.

### Feature 6: Blog & Events
CMN publishes health advocacy articles and organises community events (webinars, health drives, town halls). Accessible to both registered users and the general public without an account.

### Feature 7: Educational Resources
A library of guides covering medical malpractice, medication safety, mental health rights, and consumer protection under Nigerian law.

### Feature 8: Admin Control Panel
A dedicated admin dashboard with nationwide violation analytics, full violations table with filtering and search, Excel export, blog and event management, and live chat management.
